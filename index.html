who we are

Kuzakids organization is dedicated to supporting kids and youths in both refugee and host communities through sports and arts activities. Join us as we make a positive impact through the transformative power of sports and arts.





Our History

Kuzakids organization was formed in October 2022 with the goal of supporting kids and youths in both refugee and host communities through sports and arts activities. Despite being relatively new, we are driven by a deep passion to make a positive impact in the lives of these young individuals. We understand the unique challenges they face and are committed to providing them with a safe and nurturing environment where they can grow, express themselves, and build essential life skills.
Though we may be young as an organization, our dedication and enthusiasm drive us to create meaningful opportunities for these kids and youths.

Our Foundation

At Kuzakids, we firmly believe in the importance of caring for children, protecting their welfare, and preparing them for the future. Our foundation is built on the principles of compassion, inclusivity, and empowerment. We strive to create meaningful opportunities for every child, regardless of their background or circumstances.
Through our dedication and enthusiasm, we aim to make a lasting impact on the lives of young individuals, helping them realize their full potential and become active contributors to their communities.

Our Mission

"At Kuzakids, our mission is to empower children and youths in Kenya's Refugee and Host Communities. Through sports like basketball and football, and nurturing artistic talents, we create a safe environment fostering essential life skills, self-confidence, and belonging. We're committed to instilling teamwork, discipline, and resilience, promoting a brighter future."

Our vision

Our vision is to inspire young individuals in Kenya's Refugee and Host Communities to be physically active and creatively expressive.

Our organization has partnered with local schools to bring sports and arts programs to underserved communities. Through this collaboration, we aim to provide opportunities for children to develop their skills, foster teamwork, and promote creativity. Together, we're making a positive impact on young lives.
